# BIFROST Trademark and Brand Policy

BIFROST is open-source software, but the BIFROST and JourdanLabs names are not
open-source brand assets.

You may:

- Use the BIFROST name to truthfully describe that your project uses, forks, or
  interoperates with BIFROST.
- Link to the official repository at `https://github.com/jourdanlabs/bifrost`.
- Preserve required copyright, license, and NOTICE attribution.

You may not, without prior written permission from JourdanLabs:

- Present a fork, modified build, packaged extension, hosted service, or
  commercial offering as an official BIFROST or JourdanLabs product.
- Sell, publish, or distribute a modified version using the BIFROST name,
  BIFROST EDGE name, JourdanLabs name, Baby PULSAR mascot, or official logos in
  a way that suggests endorsement or ownership by JourdanLabs.
- Remove or obscure attribution to JourdanLabs from redistributed copies.
- Register confusingly similar names, domains, package names, extension-store
  listings, or social handles.

Recommended naming for forks:

- Good: `Acme Verify, based on BIFROST`
- Good: `Acme's fork of BIFROST`
- Not okay: `BIFROST Pro`
- Not okay: `Official BIFROST Enterprise`
- Not okay: `JourdanLabs BIFROST by Acme`

This policy is about brand trust and attribution. It does not reduce the rights
granted by the Apache License, Version 2.0 for the source code itself.
